Ingredients:
	1 cup all-purpose flour
	3/4 cup granulated sugar
	1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
	1 tsp baking powder
	1/4 tsp sea salt
	1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
	1/4 cup maple syrup
	1/4 cup almond milk
	1/4 cup vegetable oil
	1 tsp vanilla extract
	1/2 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
1. Whisk together dry ingredients.

2. Whisk together wet ingredients and combine with dry ingredients.

3. Place batter in an 8inch by 8inch greased pan and bake at 350 for 35 minutes.
